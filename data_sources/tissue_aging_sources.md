# Tissue-Specific Aging Sources

Phase 5 needs quantitative age-associated expression effects, not only
categorical up/down annotations.

Initial target tissues:

- blood
- brain or neural tissue
- liver
- muscle
- fibroblast or skin

Selection criteria:

- human data preferred for the first pass;
- quantitative differential expression or model coefficients available;
- clear age contrast or continuous age model;
- gene identifiers mappable to HGNC symbols;
- public download path and license compatible with local analysis;
- small enough to pull directly, or documented subset access if larger than
  10 GB.

Candidate datasets should be added here only after source, size, access, and
license checks are recorded.
